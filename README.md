Instructions to stream MacBook screen to iPhone

  1. On your **MacBook**, open [https://vdo.ninja/?push=WwrRPQA](https://vdo.ninja/?push=WwrRPQA) and select the screen you want to stream.
  2. On your **iPhone (Safari)**, open [https://deviamar.github.io/pupil-streaming](https://deviamar.github.io/pupil-streaming)
  3. Tap the **Share** button in Safari and select **Add to Home Screen**.
  4. Open the newly added app from your Home Screen.
  5. Rotate the iPhone to **landscape mode** to view the streamed MacBook screen.
